My name is Yu Cao, third year PhD candidate at University of Southampton.

My research interests are in the field of long-term self-sustained autonomous system. I am currently working on two related areas: theoretical research on the hybrid renewable energy system (HRES) and applied research on the autonomous maritime robotics.

The theoretical research on HRES covers the design, optimisation and management of power system on moving platforms such as ships, cars and airplanes. I extended my research interests to the autonomy of maritime robotics with a particular emphasis on sailing boats.

You can find more about my research on the left columns.

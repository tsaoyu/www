My name is Yu Cao, a PhD candidate in Fluid Structure Interactions research group at University of Southampton.

I am a researcher on hybrid renewable energy for ocean systems, also, an enthusiast on maritime robots.

I wish in the future those work could help transportation industry become safer, smarter and greener.

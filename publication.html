Softwares

\(D^3 HRE\)

\(D^3 HRE\) (Data Driven Dynamic Hybrid Renewable Energy (D3HRE) is a software suite to study hybrid renewable energy system on moving platforms. This package enables the simulation, optimisation and management of spatial-temporal hybrid renewable energy system e.g. on ships, airballons and airplanes.

PyResis

PyResis is a Python semi-empirical ship propulsion resistance estimation package. Well documented and tested against some towing tank cases.

synthetic_solar

Synthetic solar is a battery included synthetic hourly solar radiation generation tool. This package is able to generate hourly solar radiation at location with given latitude and longitude.
